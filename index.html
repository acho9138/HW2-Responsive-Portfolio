About Me

Hi! My name is Anna. I was born and raised in Sydney by my awesome parents from Korea. I've been alive for 25 years now and already went through my quarter life crisis which was mainly due to my career.

I graduated from the University of Sydney in 2017 with a Bachelor of Pharmacy and went though one brutal year of interning to become a pharmacist. Was it worth it? Not really.

But asides from my career, I've been so lucky to have been able to marry my soul mate. We grew up together and helped each other out in times of need. We ate until our stomaches burst and laughed until our guts were everywhere. As you can see, I LOVE food. I have no regrets on how much money I spent to experience all the marvelous flavours.
