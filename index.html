About Me

Hi! My name is Anna. I was born and raised in Sydney by my awesome parents from Korea.

I graduated from the University of Sydney in 2017 with a Bachelor of Pharmacy and developed many valuable life skills including:

  • Effective communication
  • Problem solving
  • Time management
  • Learning on the job
  • Patience

Being a pharmacist for almost two years, I got the chance to help many people in the community. It was both rewarding and challenging.

However, never knowing for certain what I wanted in my career and an interest in technology, I came across the Codiing Bootcamp at the University of Sydney. It was the program which allowed me to continue working as a pharmacist while learning to code to help me with the career change.
